# AXIOM Insurance Manager — Marketing content pack

## Cover

**Headline:** Your program in one workspace.

**Subhead:** Faster intake. One source of truth. Broker-ready communication.

**One line:** Not a chatbot bolted onto insurance — an operations command center for the insured.

---

## Slide 1 — Portfolio Overview (AI mode)

**Headline:** All coverages in one view.

**Body:** Policy summary across GL, Auto, Property, Workers Compensation, and Umbrella. Effective dates, carriers, limits, exposure counts, and premiums — without spreadsheet archaeology.

**Proof in UI:** MODE toggle on AI; Policy summary tab; Exposure workbook and BOR letters actions; Exposures by Coverage below.

**Buyer value:** Executives and risk teams see the whole program in minutes.

---

## Slide 2 — Portfolio Overview (Manual mode)

**Headline:** Your process. Your controls.

**Body:** Download per-line instructions aligned to Vault JSON schema. Run your agent on policy PDFs. Upload structured JSON per coverage. Same Portfolio Overview and deliverables as full AI import.

**Buyer value:** Hybrid automation — speed when you want it, auditability when you need it.

---

## Slide 3 — Exposures (General Liability)

**Headline:** Exposure data where underwriters expect it.

**Body:** Locations, GL exposures, and Raw JSON — sortable tables tied to the same session the Assistant and Broker Connect use.

**Buyer value:** Fewer re-keying errors before renewal and endorsement season.

---

## Slide 4 — Exposures (Auto + NHTSA)

**Headline:** Catch VIN errors before the broker does.

**Body:** NHTSA VIN Check compares decoded make, model, and vehicle type to your schedule. Strikethrough shows what was wrong; one click accepts the correction.

**Buyer value:** Cleaner fleet data → fewer endorsement round-trips.

---

## Slide 5 — Exposures (Workers Compensation)

**Headline:** Payroll and class codes you can total.

**Body:** State, class code, description, basis, and exposure amount by row — with grand total payroll across the book.

**Buyer value:** WC exposure visible like the underwriter sees it.

---

## Slide 6 — Broker Connect (start)

**Headline:** One endorsement transaction across the portfolio.

**Body:** Endorsements, COI requests, and Applications in one module. Session rail lists every imported line. Start one transaction; stack every change; send one email.

**Buyer value:** Endorsements that took days of attachments become one structured request.

---

## Slide 7 — Broker Connect (edit)

**Headline:** Edit exposures in context.

**Body:** Pick GL, Auto, Property, WC, Umbrella, or Account. Edit tables inline. Footer reminds you: changes stack into one broker request.

**Buyer value:** No more “see attached spreadsheet version 7.”

---

## Slide 8 — Broker Connect (stack changes)

**Headline:** Nothing vague in the request.

**Body:** Before send, review every part: named insureds removed/modified, GL locations changed, Auto locations updated, umbrella underlying adjusted — with effective date, contact, internal reference, and narrative.

**Buyer value:** Brokers get clarity; you get faster turnaround.

---

## Slide 9 — Broker Connect (confirm & send)

**Headline:** Confirm once. Send once.

**Body:** Reference, workspace, part count, auto-attached endorsement slip PDF, and Confirm & send email to your saved broker contact.

**Buyer value:** Formal request + audit trail without leaving the app.

---

## Slide 10 — Mailman (inbox)

**Headline:** Insurance email inside the workflow.

**Body:** Powered by Nylas · Outlook. Inbox, Sent, Spam. Search and unread filter. COI threads beside the same imports sidebar you use for policies.

**Buyer value:** Communication is part of operations — not a separate Outlook tab.

---

## Slide 11 — Mailman (compose)

**Headline:** Compose without leaving AXIOM.

**Body:** Pick a thread or start a new message. Attach, tag, reply — outbound tracker shows COI-1 · END-1 next steps.

**Buyer value:** Traceable broker and certificate traffic.
